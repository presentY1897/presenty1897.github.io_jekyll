### 물리적 속성(Physical Properties)

1. top
1. right
1. bottom
1. left

### 논리적 속성(Logical Properties)

1. inset-block-start
1. inset-block-end
1. inset-inline-start
1. inset-inline-end
1.

1. relative
1. absolute
1. sticky
